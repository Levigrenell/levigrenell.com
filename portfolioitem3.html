CoffeeCuppa

CuppaCoffee.

So, what is it?

They just serve an exceptional classic cup of joe. No messing around with silly names for drink sizes that "they" want us to use (if you are reading this and you like silly names for drink sizes, I'm not sorry I personally think it's silly that I get eye rolls when we both know what I want).

Don't get me started on tipping!

The smell of fresh brewed coffee in the air on a sweater-weather kind of Autumn day. CoffeeCuppa is a third place for those that need to get out of the house and out of work mode. An atmosphere so relaxing that an individual feels more at home than being actually at home.

Logo created in Illustrator, Mockups created in Dimension
