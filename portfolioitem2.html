...Ya Dig?

Do ya?

So, what is it?

YaDig? Jazz Radio focuses on the collaboration of jazz, honors and respects its predecessors as YaDig? Jazz Radio understands that it sits on the shoulders of giants.

YaDig? hosts a wide variety of differing types of jazz music, from the standards to the contemporary hits.

YaDig? finds its signal pulsing late at night at the smoke-filled bars to that romantic Sunday afternoon cruise through the countryside.

Music is such a beautiful human thing that should be protected at all costs. YaDig? preaches that to the choir as well as other patrons that are interested in getting in touch with their jazzy selves.

Developing this logo and vinyl bus wrap went through fifty different sketches.
