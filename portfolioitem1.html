This is EARTHWORLD!

Here, you can...

  • Learn how to survive.
  • Develop new team building skills.
  • Enjoy quality food cooked out in the wilderness using ingredients that you, yourself foraged or hunted for yourself.
  • Build encampments for yourself and your family all while being able to learn your way up to being completely sustainable.

So, what is it?

It's not your 'usual' amusement park. It's way more than that. A logo based off of an earthrise, depicted by this photo taken on December 24 1968 by apollo astronaut William Anders. I wanted to design a place where someone could feel free to be themselves and allow them a bit more than just a simple camping experience. People want to learn how to survive in the wilderness and to have fun with it!
